Images
Introduction

Images can be very tedious and irritating at some times. But don't you worry we have you covered!

Default Image

By default Image will have a height and widht of 9.375 rem wuth some margins around it

Round Image

You can also convert your image to a round image by simply adding img-round class on your img tag.

Sizes

There are three sizes i.e. small, medium and large in which you can show your images as your need. Medium size is default.

Responsive Image

Responsiveness is awesome! We all know it. To make your life easy you can create a responsive image which will adjust its size according to its parent container by simply adding img-res class in your tag. Try resizing your window to see the effect.
