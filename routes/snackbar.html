Introduction

A snackbar, also known as Toast, is typically a short message shown to the user at the bottom of the screen. It normally fades away after few seconds.

Snackbar

A snackbar will have a message and am action item at the end. It can also have icons instead of any buttons. By default, it's position will fixed at the bottom
